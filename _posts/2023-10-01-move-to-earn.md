The Move to Earn (M2E) Sport Data Hackathon was organized by the Ministry of Digital Affairs in 2023. I represented H2U in this 6-month data hackathon, which aimed to promote data altruism and facilitate the cross-industry application of sport technology. By the end of the project, over 1 million people had participated in the events.
