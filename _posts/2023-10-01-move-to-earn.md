The Move to Earn (M2E) Sport Data Hackathon was a corporate competition focused on sport technology, promoting cross-industry collaboration and data altruism. I represented H2U as a finalist, earning a **Distinction Award** and a prize of **500,000 TWD**

Co-hosted by the Ministry of Digital Affairs and the Institute for Information Industry (III), the event also benefited from their support in funding and a data altruism platform.  

Representing H2U, I competed against numerous corporate teams and emerged as a finalist, earning a **Distinction Award** and a prize of **500,000 TWD**.
