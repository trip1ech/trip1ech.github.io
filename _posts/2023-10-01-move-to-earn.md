The Move to Earn (M2E) Sport Data Hackathon was a corporate competition focused on sport technology, promoting cross-industry collaboration and data altruism. I represented H2U as a finalist, earning a **Distinction Award** and a prize of **500,000 TWD**

After de-indentification process, the data were uploaded to the Sport Data Altrusim Service platform (https://www.data-sports.tw/#/SportData/Landing?redirect=%2FDashboard) to help research related to hiking behavior or sport science.


The largest hikes recording app "Hiking Notes" was used to record GPS data of hiking/walking.
